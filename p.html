About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Gaurab Chand and I am a UI/UX designer currently working at 21st Tech Pvt. Ltd. With 1 year of experience in the industry, I am passionate about creating innovative and visually appealing digital experiences that delight users. At 21st Tech Pvt. Ltd, I work closely with clients and cross-functional teams to understand user needs, identify design problems, and create effective solutions. I am involved in every stage of the design process, from research and wireframing to prototyping and testing. Through collaboration and iteration, I am able to deliver user-centric designs that meet business goals and drive results. Throughout my career, I have earned a reputation for my attention to detail, strong communication skills, and ability to translate complex ideas into simple, intuitive designs. I am skilled in a variety of design tools, including Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and In Vision, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and technologies. In addition to my professional work, I am also passionate about giving back to the community. I volunteer my time to mentor and teach design to aspiring designers, and I am involved in various design and tech communities. Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio.
